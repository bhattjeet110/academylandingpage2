The Opportunity

Why Medical Tourism?

Medical Tourism is one of the fastest-growing industries globally. India has become a preferred destination for international patients due to:

Government of India Initiatives

  • Heal in India Initiative — promoting India as a global healthcare destination
  • E-Medical Visa & E-Medical Attendant Visa facilities
  • Promotion under the Ministry of Tourism
  • Support from the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare
  • Promotion through "Incredible India" campaign

Industry Growth Highlights

  • India is among the top global destinations for medical tourism
  • Patients travel from Africa, Middle East, SAARC & Asia
  • 60–80% lower treatment costs vs Western countries
  • Continuous increase in international patient inflow yearly
  • Mumbai is a leading city for international treatments
